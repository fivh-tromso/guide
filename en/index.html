Sustainable Guide Tromsø

Connect with sustainability focused places and activities in Tromsø. Discover second hand stores, loan and rental services, repairers, nature-based experiences, green communities and events.

This guide is created by volunteers and community members and is not exhaustive. Which means YOU help shape and form this guide, together with the guys in Framtiden i våre hender (FIVH) local chapter Tromsø. We value transparancy and dislike greenwashing.

We aim to update on the 1st of every month.

This webpage is based on open source code, original code by Pierre Beauguitte, Oslo student chapter, further developed by Martin Haug, Tromsø local chapter.

How can you help? Want to help with IT-development? Become a sustainability ambassador? Promote stories about the folks featured in the guide as our editor? Add new places? You can simply help us spread the guide, with friends, or hang up our pre-made posters where you live. We want to hear from you, on mail or our Fiksekvelds!

You can find FIVH Tromsø and our activities on Facebook / Instagram / framtiden.no. Direct correspondence with FIVH Tromsø via tromso@framtiden.no
